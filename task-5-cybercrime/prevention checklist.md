## 🔐 Stay Safe Online Checklist 

### ✅ Prevention Tips

- **Use strong and unique passwords** for all accounts (avoid using the same password everywhere).
- **Enable two-factor authentication (2FA)** on important apps like email, banking, and social media.
- **Be cautious of phishing messages/emails** – Do not click on unknown links or download suspicious attachments.
- **Limit personal information sharing online** – Avoid posting your phone number, address, or college ID publicly.
- **Download apps only from trusted sources** like Google Play Store or Apple App Store.
- **Log out from shared/public computers** (like in libraries or labs) after use.

### 💰 Financial / UPI Safety Tips

- **Never share your UPI PIN, OTP, or bank details** with anyone, even if they claim to be from a bank.
- **Always verify payment requests before approving** – Scammers may send “collect requests” instead of payments.
- **Use official apps only for transactions** (like Google Pay, PhonePe, Paytm) and avoid unknown payment links.

### 🚨 Reporting Cyber Crime

- **Report online fraud or cybercrime immediately at:** https://www.cybercrime.gov.in  
- **Call the Cyber Crime Helpline: 1930** (for immediate assistance in financial fraud cases)

### ⚠️ Quick Reminder
> Stay alert, think before you click, and never trust unknown sources online.
