## ✅ Responsible Social Media Use Checklist 

### ✔️ Do’s
- **Think before you post** – Ensure your content is respectful and appropriate.
- **Protect your privacy** – Keep personal information like phone numbers and addresses secure.
- **Use strong passwords** – Enable two-factor authentication for better security.
- **Verify information before sharing** – Avoid spreading fake news or misinformation.
- **Maintain a positive digital footprint** – Share content that reflects your values and personality positively.

---

### ❌ Don’ts
- **Don’t overshare personal information** – It can lead to identity theft or misuse.
- **Don’t engage in cyberbullying or trolling** – Always be respectful online.
- **Don’t click on suspicious links** – They may contain phishing or malware.
- **Don’t post offensive or inappropriate content** – It can harm your reputation and career.
- **Don’t spend excessive time on social media** – Balance your online and offline life.
